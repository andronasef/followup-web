<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| SESSION_SECRET | Single shared HS256 key signing both visitor and owner cookies — its compromise is total (forges both cookie types) |
| Password hash storage | responders.password_hash — must never be logged, must never be comparable via non-constant-time equality |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-11 | Spoofing | src/server/auth/session.ts | high | mitigate | jose's jwtVerify performs constant-time HS256 signature verification; a tampered or forged token throws rather than silently passing |
| T-01-12 | Information Disclosure | SESSION_SECRET | high | mitigate | Read from process.env only; session.ts throws at import time if unset or under 32 chars, forcing the operator to supply a strong secret rather than silently running with a weak/default one |

</threat_model>
